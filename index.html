ICLR Submission — All project materials collected below

Video Results

Full-resolution renderings demonstrating HOIGS reconstructions across representative human–object interaction sequences.

Abstract

Reconstructing dynamic scenes with rich human–object interaction remains challenging for Gaussian Splatting approaches. HOIGS explicitly models interaction-induced deformation by pairing a hexplane-based human baseline with a Cubic Hermite Spline object baseline and fusing them through a cross-attention HOI module. This design improves contact awareness, prevents object drift, and enhances geometry in low-texture regions. Experiments on BEHAVE, HOSNeRF, and other sequences show consistent gains over human-centric and 4D Gaussian methods.
